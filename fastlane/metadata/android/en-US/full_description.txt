Sightreading Trainer helps pianists and keyboard players build fluent music reading skills through structured, interactive exercises.

Connect a USB or Bluetooth MIDI keyboard and play along with exercises displayed on a grand staff. The app evaluates each note and chord you play in real time, giving instant visual feedback: correct notes turn green, wrong notes turn red, and missed notes are highlighted.

FEATURES

• Grand staff notation — treble and bass clef with key signatures, accidentals, and ledger lines rendered entirely on-device
• Real-time MIDI evaluation — notes are matched as you play, with per-notehead color feedback
• Multiple exercise types — single notes, intervals, triads, arpeggios, clustered chords, and sustain-pedal exercises
• Multi-key practice — select one or more major keys; the app picks a new key for each exercise
• Configurable difficulty — choose exercise length, time limit, hand mode (left / right / both), and accidental visibility
• Timed session mode — a countdown timer drives session length; completed exercises roll over automatically until time runs out
• Statistics — track your top correct and incorrect note groups across sessions
• Auto-reconnect — the app restores the MIDI connection when the device is replugged

PRIVACY

This app requires no internet connection and collects no data. All settings and statistics are stored locally on your device.

REQUIREMENTS

Android 10 (API 29) or later. A USB or Bluetooth MIDI-capable keyboard is strongly recommended but not required to explore the app.
